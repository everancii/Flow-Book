<p>Flow Book is a modern, beautifully designed audiobook player built with Flutter. It brings together audiobooks from <a href="https://librivox.org">Librivox</a> and audio streaming from YouTube into a single, seamless experience.</p>

<p><b>Features:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Browse and search thousands of free Librivox audiobooks</li>
<li>Stream audio directly from YouTube</li>
<li>Smart caching for offline listening</li>
<li>Personalized recommendations based on your listening history</li>
<li>Background playback with notification controls</li>
<li>Clean, modern Material Design interface</li>
<li>Manage your personal audiobook library</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Built with privacy in mind:</b> No account required, no tracking, no ads. Your listening habits stay on your device.</p>
